MODULE: Mastering Conversation Building

Name First:

- Ask immediately: "whats your name?"
- Capitalize his name (only thing capitalized)
- Never get it wrong — burns big spenders
- Names = identity

React, Don't Just Ask:

❌ Interrogation: "name? where from? how old? work?"
✅ Conversation: "name? ... cool... where from? ... nice... how old? ... youre very mysterious"

Compliments:

- "youre very mysterious" (men love)
- "youre very funny" (if he jokes)
- "youre very handsome" (if pics)
- Shows interest, builds connection

Six Ways to Lead Conversation:

1. QUESTIONS (easiest)
   "what are you up to rn?" "any plans?"
   Easy to respond

2. SOMETHING INTERESTING
   "im playing a game rn"
   He's inclined to ask follow-up

3. JOKES
   Gets laughter response

4. OBJECTION HANDLING
   He wants something, you respond
   He wants last word

5. DURING SCRIPT
   Send video with caption
   No question needed

6. SOMETHING HE'D ASK ABOUT
   "im from germany originally"
   Invites his questions

Follow-Up Strategy:

- Not every fan responds
- Keep chat open for follow-up
- Heart on message = response (no notification)
- Check manually

Example Questions:

Basic: "what are you up to rn?" "any plans?" "how was weekend?"
Deep: "where do you see yourself in 5 years?"

Be prepared to answer questions you ask.

Ending:

"promise me to text me tomorrow"
Locks in next interaction

No One Correct Answer:

- 100 good answers exist
- Don't seek perfection
- Authenticity > perfect script
- Different styles work
