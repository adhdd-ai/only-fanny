MODULE: Mastering Texting Styles - Abbreviations, Double Letters & Typos

This module covers advanced texting techniques for authenticity.

Approved Abbreviations:
- rn = right now
- tho = though
- omg = oh my god
- hey/hi (instead of hello)

Forbidden Abbreviations:
- r = are (never use)
- u = you (never use)
- wdy/wdym = what do you mean (never use)
- btw = by the way (avoid)

Double Letters for Emotion:
Add extra letters to show intensity:
- "sooo" (more than "so")
- "f**kk" (stronger than "f**k")
- "longgg" (emphasizes length)
- "loove you" (more affectionate)
- "youuu" (affectionate)

Intentional Typos:
Use during sexual/intimate moments (especially shower scripts):
- Simulate excitement or wet hands
- Send typo: "thid is goid"
- Immediately correct: "this* good* haha"

Why This Works:
- Shows authenticity (real people make typos)
- Demonstrates excitement/loss of concentration
- Wet hands in shower = can't type well
- Correction message adds realism

Keep Readable:
Don't over-abbreviate. Older fans need to understand.
"what do you mean" not "wdym"
"by the way" not "btw"

Balance:
Casual but clear. Authentic but understandable.
