MODULE: Building Customer Relationships - 3-Step Aftercare Plan

STEP 1: FINISHING THEM OFF (During Last Video)

Timing: While he's watching 2-5 minute final video, still coming

Actions:
- Encourage him to come: "i want you to come for me"
- Ask for his content: "give me that creampie" "send me a video"
- Make it mutual: "i get off watching you"
- Make him feel good about it

Why: Coming with positive feeling reduces post-nut clarity
If he doesn't come = detrimental to satisfaction

STEP 2: CONNECTION BUILDING (After Video)

Actions:
- Use heart emojis (❤️💖💗) — reward for spending
- "glad i did this with you"
- "still cant get out of my head how good that felt"
- "you made me feel so good"
- Ask about his day, interests, life
- Get to know him more
- NO SELLING

Purpose: Build emotional connection, gather info for personalization

STEP 3: FUTURE SELLING (Only If Still Responding)

Actions:
- "lets do this again"
- "lets catch up tomorrow"
- "next time will be even better"
- "promise me youll message me tomorrow... it would mean so much"

Purpose: Create expectation for next script

SKIP THIS STEP if he stopped responding — would feel transactional

Key Points:

- Three steps are sequential
- Step 1 timing is critical (during video)
- Step 2 has NO selling
- Step 3 only if engaged
- Promise for tomorrow saves follow-up work
- Next script sale made in aftercare, not next conversation
