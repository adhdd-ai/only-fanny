MODULE: Requirements Before Starting a Script

Three Requirements Before Starting:

1. TIME
Must have 30-60 minutes available

Smooth ways to ask:
- "what are you up to rn?"
- "are you alone? i wouldnt want anyone else to see"

BAD way (too obvious):
- "do you have time right now?"

If NO time:
- Continue 3-4 messages of small talk FIRST
- Then ask: "promise me youll message me when youre home"
- Don't reveal it's only about content

If pushy but no time:
- Sell nudes/smaller content instead
- Don't disappoint by refusing completely

2. INTENTION
He must WANT the script

Smooth ways to check:
- "want to see what im wearing?"
- "let me show you something"
- "i could show you something right now if you really want it"

Wait for him to confirm interest before proceeding

3. INITIATION
Create live illusion with preparation language:

- "hold on"
- "give me a second to prepare"
- "ill be right back"

Why: Makes him think you're preparing right now, creates doubt about pre-recorded content

Key Points:

- Never start script without time + intention
- Smooth questions hide transactional nature
- 3-4 messages of small talk if no time (protects authenticity)
- "Prepare" language is crucial for live illusion
- Offer alternatives rather than disappointment
