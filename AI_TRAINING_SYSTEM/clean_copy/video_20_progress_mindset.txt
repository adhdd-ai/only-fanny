MODULE: Understanding Progress in Communication

Core Mantra:

"Work IN the chat, not THROUGH the chat"

Quality Over Quantity:

❌ 5 chats/minute at 2/10 quality
✅ 1 chat/minute at 10/10 quality

Don't answer just to clear open chats.

The Three Types of Progress:

1. INFORMATION
- Ask questions
- Gather data
- Note details
- Use for future connection

2. CONNECTION
- Use past information
- Make him laugh
- Compliments
- Aftercare
- Make him fall in love

3. SELLING
- Dirty talk
- Future selling
- Script progression
- Content offers

The Balance:

Can't pursue only one type:
- No information → generic connection
- No connection → hard sell
- No selling → no revenue
- Only selling → transactional (bad)

The Path:

INFORMATION → CONNECTION → SELLING

Information feeds connection
Connection enables selling

Example of Using Past Information:

Yesterday: "my sister is in hospital"
Today: "hows your sister? is she out of hospital?"
Result: "wow she remembered" = strong connection

Every Message Must:
- Make progress (one of three types)
- Be quality, not rushed
- Move conversation forward
