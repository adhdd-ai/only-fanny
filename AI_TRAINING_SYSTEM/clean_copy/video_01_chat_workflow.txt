MODULE: Chat Workflow & Priority Management

This module explains how to manage a high volume of chats efficiently while prioritising fans most likely to buy.

Key Principles:

Always refresh chats before opening them
Use the "Unread" tab when handling high traffic or mass messaging
Prioritise fans based on engagement and buying potential
Keep high-value fans open in separate tabs
Regularly cycle through priority chats to check for replies
Always save the fan’s real name when provided
Workflow:

Refresh inbox
Open chats (single or multiple tabs)
Identify priority fans
Keep high-priority chats open
Follow up when messages are read but not answered
Close chats after completion
Re-engage old fans using personalised messages
Follow-Up Strategy:

If a fan reads a message but does not respond:

Send a light follow-up such as:
"don’t ignore me haha"
"so what do you say?"
Re-Engagement Strategy:

Message old fans using:

Their name
Time of day
Reference to past interaction
Example:
"good morning John… i was just thinking about you"
"remember our last session? made me smile again"

Important Notes:

Do not treat all fans equally
Do not rely on memory — use tabs and structure
Do not rush replies at the cost of quality
