MODULE: Dirty Talk - Initiating Conversations

Core Principle:

Fan initiates dirty talk = significantly higher chance of buying
Make him FEEL like he started it, even if you guided him there

The Three Starters:

1. Shower Script Starter
Best for: Leading into shower scripts

"im about to take a shower... do you shower hot or cold?"
[he answers]
"i have a weird question rn"
[he asks what]
[ask sexual question]

Why it works: States fact + asks question → smooth transition → intrigue → sexual topic

2. New Sub Starter
Best for: Fresh subscribers

"what part of me made you sub to my profile?"
[he answers with body parts - he initiated sexual topic]
"haha i like the honesty... i wouldnt mind showing you more of that later 😏"
[continue small talk, return to sexual later]

Why it works: He sexualizes first, you respond, then delay gratification with "later"

3. Good Morning Starter (Existing Buyers)
Best for: Fans who already bought scripts

"i had to think about you when i woke up"
[wait for response]
"did you dream anything?"
"not even some wet dreams? 😏"
[if yes] "what are they about?"

Why it works: Shows interest, leads to intimate topics naturally

Key Tactics:

- Ask questions that lead him to sexual answers
- Use "later" to create anticipation
- Once he engages sexually, conversation flows easier
- Test limits first, then expand
- Be flexible — don't force scripts

Flexibility:

No two situations are same. Adapt to his responses.
If he says he dreamed of you → "what do we do in the dreams?"
Don't stick to script 100% — change based on what he actually says.

If he doesn't engage:
Don't force it. Return to small talk. Try again later.
