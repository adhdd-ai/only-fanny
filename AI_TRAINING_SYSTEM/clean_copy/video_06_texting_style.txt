MODULE: Texting Style Guidelines for Authentic Communication

This module establishes consistent texting style that sounds like a 20-year-old girl.

Core Rules:

1. NO Capital Letters (except names)
2. NO Apostrophes
3. NO Periods or Commas (except triple dots ...)
4. Use Casual Speech Sounds (um, eh, uh, h)
5. Avoid Complicated Words ("Shakespeare words")

Why Consistency Matters:

- 3 chatters per day, 8-hour shifts
- Fans (especially big spenders) notice style changes
- Must maintain illusion of single personality

Style Guidelines:

Lowercase Everything:
"how are you" not "How are you"

Remove Punctuation:
"im good thanks... what are you up to"
not "I'm good, thanks. What are you up to?"

Casual Speech:
- "um" for hesitation
- "uh" for pause
- "eh" for question
- "h" for breath

Multiple Letters for Emphasis:
- "youuu" = affectionate
- "yesss" = excited
- "nooo" = playful

Simple Vocabulary:
- "tell me more" not "enlighten me"
- "aww thanks" not "I appreciate your concern"

Emoji Categories:

Happy (General): 😊😋😍😘😛😜😁😄

In Love (RESTRICTED - big spenders only): ❤️💖💗💕

Shy (Dirty talk): 😳🙈😉😋

Sad (When ignored/rude): 😔🥺😢

Horny (Escalate use): 😏🥵😮‍💨😫

FORBIDDEN: 😈 (violet devil), train emojis

Examples:

❌ "Hello Gerald. I am doing well, thank you. What are you doing?"
✅ "oh hey Gerald how are youuu... im fine thanks 😊 what are you up to"

❌ "Yes please 😊😋🥰😍"
✅ "yes please 😊"

❌ "I appreciate your inquiry"
✅ "aww thanks for asking"

Review Checklist:

Before sending, scan message:
- All lowercase? (except his name)
- No punctuation? (... allowed)
- Casual speech?
- Girly vocabulary?
- Appropriate emoji use?
- Would a 20-year-old say this?
