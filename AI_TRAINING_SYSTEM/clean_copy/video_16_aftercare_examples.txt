MODULE: Aftercare Examples Review

Example 1: Fan Already Came

Situation: "that did the trick... during my workout" (already came)

Adaptation:
- Skip Step 1 (finishing)
- Go directly to Step 2

Execution:
"made me lose my mind... youre really something"
"so glad i shared something special with you"
"thanks for making me come... glad i helped your stressful day"
"cant wait to come with you again... dont forget to message me"

Result: Good emotional connection, future selling included

---

Example 2: Fan Tries to End + Proactive Follow-up

Situation: Fan says "catch you later" (trying to end)

Response:
- Try to continue: "wish we could do this in the future"
- Accept end: "good night... dont forget to message me"
- Document for follow-up

Proactive Follow-up (12 hours later):
"hey james, quick question... when is your birthday? asking for a friend 😏"

Why This Works:
- Shows you remembered him
- Not transactional
- Creates falling in love effect
- Document birthday for future message on that date

Future Birthday Message:
"remember when i asked for your birthday... we had so much fun that day... still cant get it out of my head... what are you doing?"

Result: Locks in big spender

---

Example 3: Fan Left During Step 1

Situation: Fan leaves during dirty talk/Step 1

Response:
"looks like you already left... thank you for doing this with me... i really enjoyed it"

Adaptation:
- Skip Step 3 (no future selling)
- Only Step 2 (connection)

Why: Future selling inappropriate if he left early

---

Key Lessons:

Adapt Steps:
- Already came → Skip Step 1
- Left during Step 1 → Skip Step 3
- Left during Step 2 → Can still do Step 3

Proactive Follow-ups:
- Hours later (12+)
- Simple personal questions
- Document details
- Creates big spenders

Coordination:
- Tell next chatter to send proactive message
- Or document for yourself next shift
