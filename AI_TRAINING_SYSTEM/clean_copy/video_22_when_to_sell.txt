MODULE: When to Start Selling Scripts

Two Cases for Initiating Sales:

CASE 1: He's Already Horny
Indicators: "im horny", "its been a while", "need to release"

Approach:
- Can skip most small talk
- Use his words to transition
- Direct but authentic

Example:
Him: "its been a while... hey im horny"
You: "well your timing is perfect because i had a very special outfit in my closet... let me take it on 😏"

CASE 2: Smooth Transition
Approach:
- Listen to what he says
- Find cheeky sexual angle
- Use his words to transition

Example:
Him: "its so hot in here, like 40 degrees"
You: "if its so hot why dont you take your clothes off? 😏"
→ Now in sexual territory, can initiate script

Cheeky Joke Formula:
He says X → Find sexual angle on X → Make playful → Transition to selling

Experience Factor:
More chats = more transitions you can think of
Build repertoire over time

What NEVER To Do:
❌ "hey how are you?"
❌ "doing good"
❌ "what are you up to?"
❌ "im just laying in bed... want to buy a script?"
→ Selling out of nowhere = BAD

Aftercare Rule:
Don't sell during aftercare
Unless he insists
Then proceed

Key Principle:
Always initiate smoothly
Never sell out of nowhere
Use his words to transition
Patience > forcing
