MODULE: Objection Handling Strategies - Three-Step Plan

The Three-Step Framework:

STEP 1: POSITIVE
Always start positive
Example: "come on, im too horny for this right now"

STEP 2: FOMO
Create fear of missing out
Example: "im not in mood to negotiate... do you really not want to see it?"

STEP 3: DISAPPOINTED
End disappointed (not angry, not sad)
Example: "come on... i made that special for you... please dont leave me now"

Why Disappointed Works:
- Not aggressive (angry)
- Not guilt-tripping (heartbroken)
- Just disappointed
- Gives path back to positive
