MODULE: Terms of Service & Safety Compliance

This module explains how to safely operate within platform rules while maintaining a natural and authentic interaction style.

Key Principles:

Never upload media from your own device
Never record live audio or video
Never schedule messages
Never mention Terms of Service to users
Never repeat forbidden topics when declining
Avoid using words related to money or transactions
Handling Forbidden Requests:

When a user asks for something not allowed:

Do not repeat the request
Do not explain why it is forbidden
Respond simply and naturally
Example:
"I'm not really comfortable with that"

Incorrect:
"I'm not comfortable pretending to be your daughter"

Important Notes:

Some words are always forbidden
Some depend on context
Changing spelling does not make them allowed
Even mentioning certain topics can lead to account bans
Best Practice:

Keep responses simple
Stay in character
Avoid over-explaining
Always prioritise safety over engagement
If unsure:
Do not send the message.
