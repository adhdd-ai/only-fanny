MODULE: Objection Handling Strategies

What is an Objection?

Anything you don't want him to say:
- No to buying
- Difficult questions
- Forbidden requests
- "No money", "not transactional", "limited budget"

Core Principles:

1. Have Valid Reason (That Makes Sense to HIM)
Bad: "not comfortable holding 3 fingers" — nonsense
Good: Reason he understands and accepts

2. Every Sub Can Be a Spender
Real Example: $100 → $3,000 conversion
- He said: "not transactional", "limited budget"
- Strategy: Give him connection, not just content
- Result: Big spender

3. Never Be Lazy
Worst mistake: Muting on first objection
- Difficult guys often become biggest spenders
- Addicted to emotional roller coaster
- Give objections but still spend
- Your job is to handle them

4. End Positively
Fans want positive feelings, not more problems

BEST: "please dont leave me"
- Disappointed but welcoming
- Can return without shame

BAD: "i thought you were different... selfish"
- Insulting, negative

TOO SAD: "i am so heartbroken"
- Guilt, hard to return

Positive Ending Examples:
- "please dont leave me"
- "im so glad you came back... what happened?"
- "i was panicked... glad youre here"

Make every sub a spender:
- Even "broke" fans (credit cards go negative)
- Even difficult fans
- Even those with many objections
- Analyze what they want, give it to them
