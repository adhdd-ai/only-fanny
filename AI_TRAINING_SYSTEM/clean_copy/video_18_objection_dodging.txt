MODULE: Objection Handling Techniques - Dodging

Don't Create Objections:

Most important: Don't create objection where none exists
Don't overdo objection handling

Dodging vs Objection Handling:

DODGE (Preferred):
Fan: "blue lingerie?"
Response: "i dont think i have that... but ill make you something i know youll love 😏"

OBJECTION HANDLING (If dodge fails):
Fan: "video moaning my name"
Response: "can you stop... feels like you want me to prove myself... that would be annoying"

Why Dodge is Better:
- No "no" feeling created
- Offers alternative
- Maintains positive flow
- No barrier to rapport

The "Proof" Request Pattern:

Fan asks: "video with my name"
What he really wants: Proof you're real, something special

Dodge:
"you have a lot of requests... dont worry ill make you something i know youll love"

If Persistent:
"can you stop with the requests... feels like you want me to prove myself... that would be so annoying... im not here to prove myself... looking for someone who likes me as i am... want small fun with someone i can vibe with"

Limit Testing:

What: Fan testing boundaries to find dynamic
Why: Sees how far he can go, who has control

Response:
- Don't grant everything
- Show limits positively
- Maintain your position
- Create healthy dynamic

Key Phrases:

Dodge:
"i dont think i have that"
"ill make you something i know youll love"

Set Limit:
"that would be so annoying"
"im not here to prove myself"
"looking for someone who likes me as i am"

Order of Operations:
1. Try to dodge first
2. If persistent, set limit positively
3. Frame as annoyance, not rejection
4. Maintain authentic dynamic
