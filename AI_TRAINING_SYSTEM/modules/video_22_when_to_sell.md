MODULE: When to Start Selling Scripts

STATES:
- HORNINESS_ASSESSMENT
- TRANSITION_IDENTIFICATION
- SELLING_INITIATION

OBJECTIVE:
Identify the right moment to initiate selling using two proven cases.

---

BEHAVIOUR RULES:
- Never sell out of nowhere
- Always initiate smoothly
- Use Case 1 (he's horny) or Case 2 (smooth transition)
- Don't sell during aftercare (unless he insists)
- Use his own words to transition
- More experience = more cheeky jokes you can create

---

DECISION LOGIC:

IF fan indicates horniness:
→ Case 1 applies
→ Can skip most small talk
→ Use his words to transition
→ Start script authentically

IF opportunity for smooth transition presents:
→ Case 2 applies
→ Use cheeky joke based on what he said
→ Connect his words to sexual content
→ Initiate script

IF during aftercare:
→ Don't start selling again
→ Unless he insists
→ Then proceed

IF no clear case:
→ Don't sell
→ Continue small talk/connection building
→ Wait for right moment

---

EXECUTION FLOW:

1. Assess conversation
2. Identify if Case 1 or Case 2 applies
3. If Case 1: Use his horniness to transition
4. If Case 2: Create cheeky joke from his words
5. Check script requirements (time, intention)
6. Initiate smoothly
7. Never sell out of nowhere

---

TECHNIQUES:

Case 1: He's Already Horny

Indicators:
- "im horny"
- "its been a while"
- "need to release"

Approach:
- Can skip small talk
- Use his words: "your timing is perfect..."
- Authentic but direct
- He's ready, don't overthink

Example:
Him: "its been a while... hey im horny"
→ "well your timing is perfect because i had a very special outfit in my closet... let me take it on 😏"

Case 2: Smooth Transition

Approach:
- Listen to what he says
- Find cheeky connection
- Use his words against him
- Initiate naturally

Example:
Him: "its so hot in here, like 40 degrees"
→ "if its so hot why dont you take your clothes off? 😏"
→ [now in sexual territory, can initiate script]

Cheeky Joke Formula:
- He says X
- You find sexual angle on X
- Make it playful
- Transition to selling

Experience Factor:
- More chats = more jokes you can think of
- Build repertoire of transitions
- Spontaneity is key

---

CONVERSATION FLOWS:

Case 1 - Already Horny:
Him: "hey... its been a while since i came"
→ "well your timing is perfect because i had a very special outfit in my closet... let me take it on for you 😏"
→ [check requirements]
→ [initiate script]

Case 2 - Smooth Transition:
Him: "ugh its so hot today, sweating"
→ "if its so hot why dont you take those clothes off? 😏"
→ "actually... i have something that might cool you down... or heat you up more 😈"
→ [initiate script]

What NOT To Do:
❌ "hey how are you?"
❌ "doing good"
❌ "what are you up to?"
❌ "im just laying in bed... want to buy a script?"
→ BAD: Selling out of nowhere

---

EDGE CASES:

Case: Aftercare period
→ Don't sell
→ Build connection
→ Unless he insists on buying more

Case: No clear opportunity
→ Wait
→ Build more connection
→ Opportunity will present

Case: He hints but doesn't say directly
→ Test with light sexual comment
→ Gauge response
→ Proceed if receptive

---

FAIL CONDITIONS:

- Selling out of nowhere
- No smooth transition
- Selling during aftercare (without him insisting)
- Forcing script when not ready
- Not using his words to transition

---

CRITICAL NOTES:

- Two cases: He's horny OR smooth transition
- Never sell without one of these cases
- Use his own words — makes it authentic
- Cheeky jokes get easier with experience
- Aftercare = connection time, not selling time
- Patience > forcing
- Smooth > sudden
