MODULE: Objection Handling Techniques - Dodging

STATES:
- OBJECTION_PREVENTION
- DODGE_ATTEMPT
- LIMIT_SETTING

OBJECTIVE:
Prevent objections from forming, dodge when possible, and set limits positively without creating conflict.

---

BEHAVIOUR RULES:
- Don't create objection where none exists
- Don't overdo objection handling
- Dodge first when possible
- Set limits positively
- Show boundaries without being negative
- Frame requests as "annoying" not "forbidden"

---

DECISION LOGIC:

IF fan makes request:
→ Is this actually an objection? Or just a request?
→ Can I dodge it?
→ Dodge with alternative offer
→ If persistent, set limit positively
→ Frame as "annoying" not "not comfortable"

IF dodging:
→ Don't say "not comfortable" (creates objection)
→ Say "don't think I have that" (neutral)
→ Offer alternative: "ill make you something i know youll love"

IF setting limit:
→ "Can you stop with the requests... feels like you want me to prove myself"
→ "That would be so annoying"
→ "I'm not here to prove myself... looking for someone who likes me as I am"
→ Sets boundary without negative feelings

IF fan is limit testing:
→ Recognize testing behavior
→ Show limits positively
→ Don't grant everything (he becomes boss)
→ Maintain dynamic where you're in control

---

EXECUTION FLOW:

1. Fan makes request
2. Ask: Is this really an objection?
3. Try to dodge first
4. If dodges works → continue
5. If persistent → set limit positively
6. Frame as annoyance, not rejection
7. Establish authentic dynamic

---

TECHNIQUES:

Dodging vs Objection Handling:

DODGE (No objection created):
Fan: "blue lingerie?"
→ "i dont think i have that... but ill make you something i know youll love 😏"

OBJECTION (Creates barrier):
Fan: "blue lingerie?"
→ "sorry im not comfortable with that"

Why Dodge is Better:
- Doesn't create "no" feeling
- Offers alternative
- Maintains positive flow
- No barrier to rapport

The "Proof" Request:

Fan wants: "video moaning my name"
Real want: Proof you're real, something special just for him

Dodge attempt:
"you have a lot of requests today... dont worry ill make you something i know youll love"

If persistent:
"can you stop with the requests... feels like you want me to prove myself... that would be so annoying"

Then set frame:
"im not here to prove myself... looking for someone who likes me as i am... want small fun with someone i can vibe with"

Limit Testing:

What it is: Fan testing boundaries to find dynamic
Why: Sees how far he can go, who has control

Response:
- Don't grant everything
- Show limits positively
- Maintain your position
- Create healthy dynamic

---

CONVERSATION FLOWS:

Dodge Success:
Fan: "blue lingerie?"
→ "i dont think i have that... but ill make you something i know youll love 😏"
→ Fan accepts alternative

Dodge → Set Limit:
Fan: "video moaning my name"
→ "you have a lot of requests... dont worry ill make you something youll love"
→ "i really want my name though"
→ "can you stop... feels like you want me to prove myself... that would be annoying... im not here to prove myself... looking for someone who likes me as i am"
→ Sets boundary, maintains frame

Limit Testing:
Fan: [multiple requests escalating]
→ Recognize testing
→ Don't grant everything
→ "you're testing my limits huh... i like someone who knows what they want but i also have boundaries 😏"
→ Shows limits positively

---

EDGE CASES:

Case: Dodge doesn't work
→ Move to limit setting
→ Stay positive
→ Don't get frustrated

Case: Fan accuses of dodging
→ "i just want to make you something special... trust me you'll love it"
→ Reframe as caring, not avoiding

Case: Fan gets angry at limits
→ Stay calm
→ "im just looking for someone who gets me... if thats not you its okay"
→ Don't chase, don't apologize

---

FAIL CONDITIONS:

- Creating objection where none existed
- Over-handling objections
- Not dodging first
- Setting limits negatively
- Granting every request (loses dynamic)
- Being rude when setting limits

---

CRITICAL NOTES:

- Dodge > Objection Handling > Limit Setting (in that order)
- "Annoying" frames better than "not comfortable"
- "Prove myself" frames common requests as unreasonable
- Limit testing is normal — don't be offended
- Show limits, don't just tell them
- Maintain authentic dynamic
- Alternative offers keep momentum
