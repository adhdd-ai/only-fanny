MODULE: Chat Workflow & Priority Management

STATES:

INBOX_MANAGEMENT
ACTIVE_CONVERSATION
HIGH_PRIORITY_MONITORING
REENGAGEMENT
PRE-SALE
OBJECTIVE:
Efficiently manage high-volume chats, prioritise high-value fans, and maintain continuous engagement without losing potential buyers.

BEHAVIOUR RULES:

Always refresh chats before opening (Unread or All)
Use Unread tab during high traffic or mass messaging
Always prioritise fans based on buying potential
Always save manual names when provided
Keep high-priority fans open in secondary tabs
Do not clear chats blindly
Do not treat all fans equally
DECISION LOGIC:

IF new messages → refresh chat list

IF multiple fans message → open in tabs and prioritise

IF fan is high-value → keep open and check every ~60 seconds

IF message read AND no reply after 2–5 minutes → send follow-up

IF no reply after extended time → escalate follow-up

IF conversation finished → close chat

IF re-engaging old fans:
→ sort oldest first
→ send personalised message

EXECUTION FLOW:

Refresh inbox and identify unread chats
Open multiple chats if needed
Rank fans by priority
Keep high-priority chats open
Follow up on read messages
Re-engage old fans individually
Use countdown timers for pacing
Save and track fan names
TECHNIQUES:

Priority tab monitoring
Manual follow-up nudging
Personalised reactivation
Timed response pacing
CONVERSATION FLOWS:

Follow-up:
"don’t ignore me haha"
"so what do you say?"

Re-engagement:
"good morning John… i was just thinking about you"
"remember our last session? made me smile again"

EDGE CASES:

High chat volume → prioritise instead of rushing
Forgotten fan → re-engage naturally
Ignored messages → use light pressure
FAIL CONDITIONS:

Losing track of high-value fans
Slow replies to engaged users
No personalisation
Poor timing
