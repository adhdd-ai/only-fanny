MODULE: Understanding Progress in Communication

STATES:
- PROGRESS_IDENTIFICATION
- TYPE_SELECTION
- QUALITY_EXECUTION

OBJECTIVE:
Make every message contribute to progress: Information, Connection, or Selling.

---

BEHAVIOUR RULES:
- "Work in the chat, not through the chat"
- Quality > quantity (1 chat at 10/10 quality > 5 chats at 2/10)
- Every message must make progress
- Three types: Information, Connection, Selling
- Can't pursue only one type — need balance
- Information → Connection → Selling (builds on each other)

---

DECISION LOGIC:

IF composing message:
→ Ask: What progress does this make?
→ Type 1: Information (gathering data)
→ Type 2: Connection (building bond)
→ Type 3: Selling (moving toward sale)
→ Every message must contribute to at least one

IF gathering information:
→ Ask questions
→ Note answers
→ Use in future for connection

IF building connection:
→ Use past information
→ Make him laugh
→ Compliments
→ Aftercare
→ Make him fall in love

IF selling:
→ Dirty talk
→ Future selling
→ Script progression
→ Content offers

IF only doing one type:
→ Will fail
→ Can't sell without connection
→ Can't connect without information
→ Can't only get information (no sale)

---

EXECUTION FLOW:

1. Identify current conversation state
2. Determine which type of progress needed
3. Craft message that contributes to that type
4. Ensure quality over quantity
5. Every message moves conversation forward

---

TECHNIQUES:

The Three Types:

1. INFORMATION
- Ask questions
- Gather data
- Note details
- Use later for connection
Example: "where are you from?" "what do you do?"

2. CONNECTION
- Use past information
- Make him laugh
- Compliments
- Aftercare
- Make him fall in love
- Emotional bond
Example: "hows your sister doing? is she out of hospital now?" (remembered from yesterday)

3. SELLING
- Dirty talk
- Future selling
- Script progression
- Content offers
- Make him buy
Example: "want to see what im wearing?"

The Balance:

Information → Connection → Selling

- Information feeds connection
- Connection enables selling
- Selling funds everything

Can't skip steps:
- No connection = hard sell
- No information = generic connection
- Only selling = transactional (bad)

Quality Over Quantity:

❌ Answering 5 chats/minute at 2/10 quality
✅ Answering 1 chat/minute at 10/10 quality

Don't clear open chats just to clear them.
Work IN the chat, not THROUGH the chat.

Using Past Information:

Yesterday: "my sister is in hospital"
Today: "hows your sister? is she out of hospital?"
Result: "wow she remembered" = connection

---

CONVERSATION FLOWS:

Progressive Build:
1. Information: "where are you from?" → "texas"
2. Connection: "oh i love texas... visited austin once, such great food"
3. Information: "what do you do?"
4. Connection: "that sounds interesting... tell me more"
5. Selling: "speaking of interesting... want to see something?"

Connection Through Memory:
Yesterday: "stressed about presentation"
Today: "how did your presentation go? hope it went well"
→ Strong connection

---

EDGE CASES:

Case: Time waster
→ Less progress per message okay
→ Don't invest heavily
→ Still make some progress or end

Case: Already has connection
→ Focus on selling
→ But maintain connection

Case: New fan
→ Focus on information + connection
→ No selling yet

---

FAIL CONDITIONS:

- Answering just to clear chats
- No progress per message
- Only one type of progress
- Forgetting to use past information
- Quality sacrificed for quantity

---

CRITICAL NOTES:

- "Work in the chat, not through the chat" = mantra
- Every message must make progress
- Three types work together
- Past information = powerful connection tool
- Quality beats quantity every time
- Information → Connection → Selling is the path
- Module 9 (this) is most important — review 2-3 times
